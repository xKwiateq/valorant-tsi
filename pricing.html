Pricing

Good news! Valorant is free to play. You can download and play the game for free. You can also purchase Valorant Points to unlock new content and customize your agent. Valorant Points can be purchased with real money or earned through gameplay. Valorant Points are not required to play the game. You can also purchase a Battle Pass to earn rewards and unlock new content. Battle Passes can be purchased with Valorant Points or earned through gameplay. Battle Passes are not required to play the game. Valorant Points and Battle Passes are only available in select regions. For more information, please visit our FAQ.
