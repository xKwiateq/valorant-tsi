About our game

Matches are played between two teams of five players. Each player can choose their character, each with a different skill set and gameplay style. Initially, the developers prepared three maps on which you can play a match - Split, Bind and Haven. In later updates, five more were added - Ascent, Icebox, Breeze, Fracture and Pearl. The servers are designed to avoid synchronization issues and ensure dynamic gameplay.
