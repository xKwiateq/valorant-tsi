Frequently asked questions

Q: What is Valorant?
A: Valorant is a free-to-play, competitive, first-person shooter developed and published by Riot Games. The game is available for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One.

Q: How do I download Valorant?
A: You can download Valorant for free on the official website.

Q: How do I play Valorant?
A: You can play Valorant for free. You can also purchase Valorant Points to unlock new content and customize your agent. Valorant Points can be purchased with real money or earned through gameplay. Valorant Points are not required to play the game. You can also purchase a Battle Pass to earn rewards and unlock new content. Battle Passes can be purchased with Valorant Points or earned through gameplay. Battle Passes are not required to play the game. Valorant Points and Battle Passes are only available in select regions. For more information, please visit our FAQ.

Q: How do I get Valorant Points?
A: You can purchase Valorant Points with real money or earn them through gameplay. Valorant Points are not required to play the game.

Q: How do I get a Battle Pass?
A: You can purchase a Battle Pass with Valorant Points or earn it through gameplay. Battle Passes are not required to play the game.
